Monoton

Monoton was designed by Vernon Adams, a designer who experimented with typeface between 2007 and 2014. Adams released Monoton, in addition to other well-known open-source fonts, including Oswald, and Pacifico (Lovehart Design). Monoton is a contemporary take on Prisma, a Metalpress font that has a similar retro style. I chose Monoton because of its distinctive “Old Hollywood” flair that calls to mind the bright lights found in the theater district where I grew up in Southern California.
